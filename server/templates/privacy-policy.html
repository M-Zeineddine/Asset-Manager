Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

LocalTreats ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the LocalTreats mobile application and related services.

Information We Do Not Collect

LocalTreats does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app does not require account creation or login. No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are used.

How the App Works

LocalTreats connects to our backend server to display local business gift cards and facilitate gifting. Any data entered (such as a recipient name for a gift) is processed solely to fulfill the gifting action and is not retained for any other purpose.

Location

The app may request access to your device's location solely to show nearby local businesses. Location data is used in real time and is never stored or transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

LocalTreats does not integrate with any third-party advertising, analytics, or data broker services.

Children's Privacy

LocalTreats is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us
